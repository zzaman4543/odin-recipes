Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a delightful dish renowned for its crispy exterior and soft, cheesy interior. This quick recipe yields a meal that is both enjoyable and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • 1 large sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup frozen corn
  • 1 (19 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 8 (8 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Boil and mash sweet potatoes
  2. Mix sweet potatoes with cilantro, chilli powder, and salt
  3. Microwave corn until warmed
  4. Assemble quesadillas by spreading sweet potato mixture, black beans, and corn on tortillas, topping with cheese, and folding.
  5. Cook quesadillas in a frying pan with cooking spray until cheese is melted and beans are heated through
  6. Repeat with remaining quesadillas
  7. Slice cooked quesadillas into quarters
