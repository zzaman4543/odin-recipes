Apple Pie

Description

Apple pie is a classic dessert with a flaky, buttery crust and sweet filling. This recipe is simple to follow, but still results in a tasty and satisfying apple pie.

Ingredients

  • 8 small Granny Smith apples
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 (9 inch) double-crust pie pastry, thawed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
  2. Melt butter, add flour, then sugars and water
  3. Prepare a 9-inch pie pan with bottom crust
  4. Roll out remaining pastry, cut into 1-inch strips
  5. Place sliced apples in bottom crust, form a mound
  6. Create a lattice crust with the strips
  7. Pour sugar-butter mixture over the lattice crust
  8. Bake at 425°F (220°C) for 15 minutes, then reduce to 350°F (175°C) and bake for 35-45 minutes more
