Brownies

Home

Description

Brownies are a sweet dessert made of chocolate. They are typically baked and cut into squares. Often, they include add-ins like nuts and chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Dark chocolate
  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • White chocolate
  • Milk chocolate
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Cut the butter into small cubes
  2. Tip the butter into a bowl
  3. Break the dark chocolate into small pieces
  4. Drop the chocolate into the bowl
  5. Fill a saucepan with hot water
  6. Rest the bowl on top, without it touching the water
  7. Allow the contents of the bowl to melt, stirring occasionally
  8. Remove the bowl from the pan
  9. Heat the oven to 180°C
  10. Line a square tin with kitchen foil
  11. Tip plain flour and cocoa powder into a sieve held over a bowl
  12. Chop white chocolate and milk chocolate into chunks
  13. Break large eggs into a bowl
  14. Add sugar to the bowl
  15. Whisk the eggs and sugar
  16. Pour the chocolate mixture over the egg mousse
  17. Gently fold them together from all sides
  18. Hold the sieve over the bowl of egg chocolate mixture
  19. Add the cocoa and flour mixture through the sieve
  20. Gently fold everything together
  21. Stir in the white and milk chocolate chunks
  22. Pour the mixture into the tin
  23. Bake for 25-30 minutes
  24. Cut into squares after cooling
