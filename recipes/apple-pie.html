Apple Pie

Home

Description

Apple pie is a sweet dish made primarily of apples. It is baked inside a pastry crust. Sugar and spices are often added for flavour. It can be served hot or cold, and with other foods like ice cream.

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Put a layer of paper towels on a baking sheet
  2. Core, peel, and slice the apples
  3. Lay the apples evenly over the baking sheet
  4. Put paper towels on top
  5. Beat the butter and sugar in a large bowl to make the pastry
  6. Mix in egg yolk
  7. Mix in flour
  8. Make the contents of the bowl into dough
  9. Mix sugar, cinnamon, and flour in a different bowl
  10. Heat the oven to 190°C
  11. Line a pie tin with the pastry
  12. Put the apples in the new bowl
  13. Mix the contents of the new bowl
  14. Pile the tin with the contents of the new bowl
  15. Lay a pastry lid over the tin
  16. Brush the pastry with the egg white
  17. Sprinkle the pastry with sugar
  18. Bake for 40-45 minutes
  19. Serve with whipped cream
