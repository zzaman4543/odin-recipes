Description

Pizza is a savoury, delicious dish originating from Italy. It is usually made of a flat dough base with cheese, tomato, and other ingredients layered on top. Ovens are the main way pizza is cooked.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Passata
  • Basil
  • Garlic cloves
  • Mozzarella
  • Cherry tomatoes

Steps

  1. Put some flour in a large bowl
  2. Add salt to the flour
  3. Pour in 200 ml of warm water
  4. Add a tablespoon of olive oil
  5. Mix the contents of the bowl to make dough
  6. Knead the dough until smooth
  7. Crush the garlic cloves
  8. Mix the passata, basil, and garlic cloves together to make sauce
  9. Season the sauce
  10. Roll out the dough
  11. Heat the oven to 240°C
  12. Smooth the sauce over the dough
  13. Scatter the mozzarella and cherry tomatoes over the dough
  14. Place the pizza in a tray
  15. Bake the pizza for 8-10 minutes
