Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese is a delicious comfort food known for its creamy texture and golden hue. This recipe is a stove-top version that uses simple ingredients, making it easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
