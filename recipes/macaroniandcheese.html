Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Macaroni and cheese is a delicious comfort food known for its creamy texture and golden hue. This recipe is a stove-top version that uses simple ingredients, making it easy to prepare. It's also versatile and can be customized through various add-ins.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) box elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil macaroni in salted water for 8 minutes
  2. While macaroni cooks, make a roux by melting butter, adding flour, salt, pepper, and stirring until melted
  3. Slowly pour in milk, stirring until smooth and bubbling
  4. Add 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese to the roux, stirring until melted.
  5. Drain macaroni and stir into the cheese sauce until coated.
